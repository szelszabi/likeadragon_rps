Lore

Goro Majima challenged Kiryu Kazuma for a duel as usual. However this duel was far from usual, you see this is a first-to-eleven match of Rock Paper Scissors. This historic duel will finally decide the strongest yakuza in the city of Kamurocho. Who will win? The reckless Dragon of Dojima or the Mad Dog of Shimano? Everything is in your hands to finalize their fates. You play as Kazuma Kiryu who is also known as the Dragon of Dojima.
